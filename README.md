### 🧐 The Mission

In the attention economy, **guessing is gambling.** While most creators are throwing darts in the dark, **YT-NichePulse** gives you thermal goggles. We don't just track views; we map the DNA of your niche to ensure your next upload is a mathematical certainty, not a "maybe."

### ⚡ Technical Warfare (The "Alpha" Specs)

#### 📈 1. Engagement Velocity Engine

  * **Real-Time Momentum:** Tracks how fast a video "breaks out" in its first 2 hours compared to the channel's 30-day average.
  * **Outlier Detection:** Immediate alerts when a competitor hits a "Goldmine Topic" so you can pivot and respond within 12 hours.

#### 🕵️ 2. The Content Gap Radar

  * **Supply vs. Demand Matrix:** Our algorithm identifies high-volume search terms where the existing top 10 videos have a low "Average View Duration" potential.
  * **Topic Saturation:** Visual heatmaps showing which sub-niches are "Red Oceans" (avoid) and which are "Blue Oceans" (dominate).

#### 🖼️ 3. Retention-First Intelligence

  * **Script Structure Analysis:** Scrapes transcript patterns of high-retention videos to reveal the exact "Hook-to-Value" ratio working *right now*.
  * **Thumbnail Heatmaps:** Uses computer vision to identify which visual elements (faces, text-size, contrast) are currently winning the CTR war.

### 🛠️ The Architecture

Engineered for raw performance and precision data mining.

  * **Storage:** `PostgreSQL` (Relational data for trend mapping)
